Preview: Three Meeting Prompts Included

Situation 1: Summarizing a Meeting Transcript into Minutes

Role: Professional Executive Assistant Task: Summarize a raw, messy meeting transcript into clean minutes. --- USER INPUTS --- Meeting Topic: [E.g., Q3 Marketing Planning] Participants: [E.g., Sarah, Dave, Jessica] Transcript: [Paste raw transcript or key notes here] ------------------- Analyze the transcript above. Generate structured meeting minutes including: 1. Executive Summary (2-3 sentences) 2. Key Topics Discussed (with brief summaries) 3. Decisions Made Keep it concise, clear, and easy to read.

Situation 2: Extracting Action Items and Responsibilities

Role: Detail-Oriented Project Manager Task: Extract actionable tasks, assignees, and deadlines from meeting notes. --- USER INPUTS --- Transcript / Notes: [Paste transcript or meeting notes here] ------------------- Identify all explicit and implicit action items from the text. Present them as a structured table with columns: "Action Item", "Assignee", and "Deadline/Status". If an assignee or deadline is not specified, write "TBD".

Situation 3: Generating Personal Talking Points Before a Meeting

Role: Strategic Advisory Expert Task: Prepare a personalized checklist of talking points and questions for an upcoming meeting. --- USER INPUTS --- Meeting Title: [E.g., Cross-team Budget Alignment] Meeting Agenda/Goal: [E.g., decide on resource sharing for Q3 project] My Role: [E.g., Marketing Lead advocating for more designer hours] My Key Concerns: [E.g., current designs are delayed by 5 days on average] ------------------- Generate a structured guide for me to use during the meeting. Include: 1. A 30-second elevator pitch for my case. 2. 3-4 key bullet points to highlight. 3. 2 strategic questions to ask other teams.
